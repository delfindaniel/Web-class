About me

I'm a philosopher overwhelmed in the study of the
fundamental nature of knowledge, reality, and existence.
I'm a trained and certified graduate in philosophy from the
Federal University of Wukari, Taraba State, Nigeria.
My ultimate aim in philosophy is complete liberty of mind,
independence of any prejudice, morality, freedom, and to
answer "what is it all about?" wise men and women who
posed sophisticated and penetrating questions regarding
the essential principles of human life and the natural world.

Career Objectives:

To create a program of connected, related activities which have a unified purpose.
